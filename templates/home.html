First Endpoint:

trips

Parameters: starttime , endtime , from_station_id , to_station_id

Example1: /trips?starttime=2013-06-28&endtime=2013-06-30
Returns the average duration for that time span

Example2: /trips?starttime=2013-06-28&endtime=2013-06-30&from_station_id=90
Returns the average duration for that time span and further filters the results by the stations that the trip started from

Example3: /trips?starttime=2013-06-28&endtime=2013-06-30&from_station_id=90&to_station_id=90
Returns the average duration for that time span further filters the results by the stations that the trip started from and ended at

Second Endpoint:

tripsbike

Parameters: bike_id , starttime , stop

Example: /tripsbike?starttime=2013-06-28&endtime=2013-06-30&bike_id=58
Returns the average duration for that time span and further filters the results by the bike_id
